{% trans 'Hosting & Data security' %}

{% blocktrans %}Shortdiary is hosted at Hetzner in Germany on infrastructure provided by the ohai.su project, a non-profit that aims to improve the web by providing open services. Your data never leaves the European Union (Unless, of course, you access shortdiary from outside the EU).{% endblocktrans %}

{% blocktrans %}All communication betweeten you and us is encrypted, if possible. All web pages under shortdiary.me automatically redirect to their SSL counterpart. We also use HTTP Strict Transport Security (HSTS) to make sure your browser always uses SSL when browsing shortdiary.me, so that even if somebody overtakes your local network, they won't be able to spoof shortdiary. All emails from us are sent using TLS encryption if your server supports it (Google Mail and most other large providers do).{% endblocktrans %}

{% blocktrans %}Access to the shortdiary servers is limited to two persons: Lukas himself and Patrick Rauscher, who is part of the ohai.su team.{% endblocktrans %}

{% blocktrans %}Oh, and by the way: If you want to review the source code, feel free to! It's open and on GitHub.{% endblocktrans %}
